Personal Project: Custom Gaming PC Build Under RM2000

Hardware Assembly Cable Management Windows 11 Installation Performance Testing Disk Management
Category Custom Computer Building
Environment Personal Workspace Setup
Focus Hands-On PC Building Experience
Status Finished
Read More

A month-long personal computer building project focused on assembling and configuring a capable mid-range workstation and gaming system within a strict RM2000 budget constraint.

The project involved researching hardware compatibility, sourcing cost-efficient components, assembling and configuring the system, installing Windows 11, organizing internal cable management, configuring storage drives, and optimizing overall system stability for long-term usage.

The completed build utilized an Intel i5-12400F and AMD Radeon RX 6600, achieving stable 60 FPS gameplay in Tekken 8 and EA Sports FC 24 at high settings, while maintaining higher frame rates in other optimized titles depending on graphical configurations.

The entire build was completed at an overall cost of approximately RM1870, while equivalent current Malaysian market pricing for the full system is estimated between RM2700 and RM3000.

The project emphasized practical technical experience, hardware compatibility understanding, thermal and airflow awareness, troubleshooting during assembly and setup, operating system deployment, and real-world PC optimization for gaming and productivity workloads.

  • Intel Core i5-12400F
  • ASUS PRIME H610M-K
  • Sapphire Radeon RX6600
  • Kingston Fury 16GB RAM (2x8GB)
  • Cooler Master MWE Gold 80 PSU
  • Apacer 256GB SSD + 1TB Seagate Barracuda HDD
  • Slevcase Chassis
  • ThinkCentre Monitor
