2025 - Miniature Patrol Car Using ROS2 & Gazebo

ROS2 • RViz2 • Gazebo • Ubuntu • Blender • Simulation

A semi-autonomous ROS 2 mobile robot simulation developed in a Gazebo-based Gotham-inspired environment. The project focuses on stable navigation, sensor-based perception, and coordinated mission control in a complex and hazardous setting. The robot integrates LiDAR and vision sensors to perceive its surroundings, interpret visual cues, and avoid dynamic obstacles while maintaining reliable autonomous behavior with optional human intervention.
